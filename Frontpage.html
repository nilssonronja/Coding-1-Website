The Trip of 2019

The Nordic country of Norway has become a popular road trip destination for the youth of Scandinavia during the recent years. Visiting the Islands introduces the traveler to endless mountains, towns, beaches, and the beautiful scenery of Norway. Many go in without planning the trip from day to day but rely on their instinct and go with the flow, finding new places to explore and new people to get to know.



Lofoten, Norway

In the year 2019 I took a trip to the Island with two friends and drove from Helsinki, Finland all the way to Å i Lofoten, the end of E10 road going through the Islands. The 10 day trip was spent driving to northern Finland, passing through Sweden, entering Lofoten and circling back following the same path. All nights were spent sleeping in a tent in various campsites and beaches which allowed for a flexible schedule and freedom.
